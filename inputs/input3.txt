Christmas is a well-known Christian holiday set in December, celebrated the world over and famed for its decorations and Santa Clause. 
The Christmas means  “Feast day of Christ”.It is a yearly celebration marking Jesus Christ’s birth; it is observed on the 25th of December 
as a cultural and religious celebration among a lot of people all over the world. Christmas is celebrated in all Christian countries but there are 

differences in the way each nation celebrates this date.
